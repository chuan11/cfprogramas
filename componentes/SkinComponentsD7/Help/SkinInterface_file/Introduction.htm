Description

Use TSkinInterface to put on a form, an advanced graphic control for designing and customizing GUI.

Advanced features for a complete control:

     

     Placement of child controls.

     Derive the graphic aspect of the control from a bitmap.

     Manage the transparence of the control.

     Manage the transparence of the Parent control.

     Manage the transparence of the MainForm.

     Manage the tolerance of the area transparent.

     Manage the translucence of the MainForm.

     Manage the color of the control.

     Manage, on automatic,  the Drag operation of the control.

     

     and more…
